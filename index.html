About Us

Coffee Haven is dedicated to bringing you the finest coffee experience with freshly brewed drinks and delicious breakfast options. Our mission is to create a warm and welcoming space for coffee lovers.Coffee Heaven is a São Paulo based, family-owned coffee brand. The current principal owners, Bruno and Camilla took over the 44 years established company from his father, and wanted to expand their high-quality coffee worldwide. Céu do Café (their previous name) decided to rebrand so that their brand could resignate with their new upcoming customers. Made using the natural dry process in a region with a warm climate, the brand produces high-quality, natural and rich in taste coffee while being sustainable and engaged in the whole process.
